Main Page
Catch as much
fish as you can in an hour! Each actions costs you time.
Bait increases your chances of getting a bite, but you can still catch fish without using it.
When you catch
a fish, your skill rises. The higher your skill, the better your chances!
Once you have a fish on the line:
Reeling in is safer. You can slowly pull the fish in, as long as it isn't too big for you!
Jerking the line
is risky. You might bringing the fish a lot closer (no matter its size) or wreck your chances.
Cut the line if this fish is taking too long!
